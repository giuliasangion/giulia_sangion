BEING WEIRD IS BETTER THAN BEING BORING

Born in Legnano (Milano) on January 16 1997, Giulia Angela Sangion is a "Graphic Designer Aspirant Tattooer" condensed in a height of 170 cm.

Graduated from Classical studies, she's studying at Politecnico of Milan since October 2016.
She attended the first year of university as Interior Desiner, but she has switched to Communication Design lately.

Metalhead since high school, while working she is used to listen her favorite Spotify playlist "Core as fuck" that includes bands as Metallica, Five Finger Death Punch, Trivium and many others.
Drawing is a stress reliever other than a work. Her drawings are generally in realistic black and white style, but sometimes the geek that lives in her takes over the pencil and everything becomes a comics.
